Adoption Application

The application is the first step to see if you are potentially the right match for the animal of interest. This is not a guarantee/commitment of adoption. Our adoption process involves a home check, adoption contract & adoption fee of $400-$600 for dogs depending on breed/age and $175 for cats. Adoption fees are non-refundable. Fees include spay/neuter, vaccines, de-wormer & microchip.

How To Begin the Adoption Process

How To Begin the Adoption Process
1) To begin the adoption process please click the buttons below. Download the form to your computer or phone.
2) Click form filling icon and fill out the application.
3) Email the adoption application to INFO@LAAR.ORG.
4) Once we receive your application we will contact you and make arrangements to introduce you to the animal you are interested in. *We will respond by email within 24 hours. If you do not receive an email, please check your junk mail.*

Please Note: **We do not hold animals. If you are planning on going out of town in the near future and cannot adopt immediately, please wait to contact us after you have returned and are ready to adopt.**
